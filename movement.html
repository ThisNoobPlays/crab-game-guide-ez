Movement

Having good movement is crucial in Crab Game, as it allows you to move faster and more smoothly.

Diagonal Bunnyhop

Whenever you sprint, you should sprint like this.

First, practice running diagonally by pressing W and A or W and D at the same time. Make sure that you are comfortable running in the direction of straight forward but moving diagonally.

The next step is to jump continuously while running diagonally. As soon as you land, press space to jump again while keeping your momentum.

By doing this, you can move magnitudes faster than someone just running straight forward.

Diagonal Ladder Climb

Whenever climbing ladders, climb diagonally by pressing W and A or W and D, as this is faster than just climbing by pressing W.

Diagonal Jump

If you need to make a large jump, you should use diagonal jumping.

This is where you jump diagonally by pressing W and A or W and D, and right before you jump, you shake your mouse back and forward again to give yourself a momentum boost.

This does take some practice to get the maximum possible distance.

Sliding

You can slide in Crab Game by holding C while moving.

This is very useful when going down a slope, because when you slide, you gain lots of speed. Additionally, you can even slide against a wall, letting you gain a little bit of speed or nullify fall damage.
